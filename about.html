What is ResQ AI?

ResQ AI is an advanced, automated AI-driven multi-track decision support matrix engineered to revolutionize modern disaster response and tactical planning protocols.

By harmonizing advanced geospatial pipelines, machine learning algorithms, and public data, we provide real-time coordination matrices for cyclone paths, heatwaves, and shelter health indexes. Our platform bridges the critical latency gap between early warning distribution and actionable community support execution.
